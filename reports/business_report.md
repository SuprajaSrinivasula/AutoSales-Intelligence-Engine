# Business Report

## Problem Statement
Automobile dealers struggle to forecast monthly unit sales.

## Business Impact
- Improved inventory planning
- Reduced stock-outs
- Data-driven decision making

## Key Insights
- Region significantly impacts demand
- Seasonal trends observed in Q3
- Certain product categories outperform others

## Recommendations
- Increase inventory before peak months
- Focus marketing on high-demand regions
- Optimize pricing for low-performing products
